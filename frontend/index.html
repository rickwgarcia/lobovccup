Two Ways to Compete

Pick Your Track

Whether you're building the next big thing or hunting for it — there's a track for you.

🚀
Founder Track

You've got a startup idea — or an early-stage company. Pitch student VC teams during Discovery Days and raise real capital. No judges picking winners. Just investors making bets.

  • Pitch to multiple VC teams
  • Compete for real investment capital
  • Access mentors and advisors
  • Build your investor materials
  • Founders win what they raise
Register as Founder View Founder Guide
💼
VC Track

You want to work in venture. Build a fund, pitch real LPs, source deals, run diligence, and deploy capital. You'll be judged on process, rigor, and conviction.

  • Pitch an LP panel for real capital
  • Source and evaluate startup deals
  • Write term sheets + deal memos
  • Build an investment track record
  • Compete for $16,000 in prizes
