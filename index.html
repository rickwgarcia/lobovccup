The Quest

What Is Lobo VC Cup?

Lobo VC Cup is UNM's premier student venture competition. Founders pitch real startup ideas to student VCs. VCs manage real funds and make real investment decisions. Everyone walks away with skills, connections, and — if you win — real prize money.

The Founder's Path

Build Your Startup

Develop your idea, pitch to student VCs, and win from the $20,000 prize pool. Your pitch is your power — the better you sell your vision, the more you raise, and the more you win.

You win what you raise →
The Investor's Path

Run a VC Fund

Manage a fund, evaluate startups, and make real investment decisions. Learn to spot opportunity, negotiate deals, and build a portfolio that outperforms the competition.

Top 3 funds win $10K, $5K, $1K →
