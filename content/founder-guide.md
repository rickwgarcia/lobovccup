# Founder Guide

## What You're Doing

You'll develop your startup idea, pitch to student VCs during Discovery Days, and win the capital you raise from a $20,000 prize pool. The better you pitch, the more you win.

---

## Timeline & What to Expect

### March 2–6: Education Week (Virtual)

Daily mentor sessions on startup fundamentals - problem validation, customer discovery, business models, MVP development, and go-to-market strategy. Attend live, watch recordings, or review materials.

### March 7: Team Registration Due

Submit your team info: [Registration Form](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1BIPgCaye6sbsHqKr79l6j_rjUNdpuu85Ro8ISw_5KDo/edit?usp=sharing)

### March 9–12: Pitch Prep (Virtual)

Work with your founder mentors to refine your pitch deck, sharpen your story, and prepare for tough VC questions.

### March 13: Pitch Deck Due

Submit your final pitch deck.

### March 25–26: Discovery Days (IN-PERSON)

This is game time. Pitch your startup to at least 2 VC teams. Expect tough questions about your business model, market size, competition, and team. VCs will conduct due diligence - be prepared.

### March 30: Investment Day (IN-PERSON)

Negotiate with VCs on investment terms - how much capital, equity stake, and deal structure. This is where you finalize your funding.

### April 3: Awards Ceremony (IN-PERSON)

Celebrate and collect your prize money.

---

## How You Win

You win the capital you raise. VCs will invest based on your pitch quality, business viability, team strength, and ability to handle due diligence.

**What VCs look for:**

- Clear problem and compelling solution
- Strong business model and path to revenue
- Understanding of your market and competition
- Credible team that can execute
- Ability to answer tough questions confidently

---

## Requirements (Must-Dos to Stay Eligible)

- ✓ Submit pitch deck by March 13
- ✓ At least one team member must attend each Discovery Day (March 25–26)
- ✓ Pitch to at least 2 VC teams during Discovery Days
- ✓ Respond to VC questions during due diligence sessions
- ✓ Attend Investment Day (March 30) and Awards Ceremony (April 3)

**Failure to pitch during Discovery Days = ineligible for prizes**
