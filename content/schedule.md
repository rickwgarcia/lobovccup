# Competition Schedule

## Lobo VC Cup 2026 — Key Dates

---

## March 2–6 | Education Week
**Format:** Virtual (Zoom)

Mandatory workshops for all participants covering venture capital fundamentals, pitch preparation, and competition rules. Sessions run evenings (times TBD — watch your email).

**Topics covered:**
- Venture capital 101 (for all participants)
- Building your pitch deck (Founders)
- Building your fund thesis and LP pitch (VCs)
- What investors look for at the pre-seed stage
- Due diligence fundamentals (VCs)
- Term sheet basics

---

## March 7 | Team Registration Deadline
**Format:** Online

All teams — Founders and VCs — must complete registration at lobovccup.unm.edu by end of day. Late registrations will not be accepted.

**Required at registration:**
- Team name
- Pathway selection (Startup or VC)
- All member names and UNM emails
- Key contact information
- Brief idea/thesis description

---

## March 9–12 | Pitch Prep Week
**Format:** Virtual (Zoom)

Work directly with faculty advisors and mentors to refine your materials. Office hours available. Sign up through the portal.

**This week:**
- Founders: Deck review sessions with advisors
- VCs: LP pitch coaching and thesis feedback
- Both: Industry mentor networking sessions

---

## March 13 | Submissions Due
**Deadline:** 11:59 PM Mountain Time

All materials uploaded through lobovccup.unm.edu submissions portal.

**Founders submit:** Pitch deck (PDF or PPTX)

**VCs submit:** LP pitch video (MP4 or MOV, 5–7 minutes)

No extensions. No exceptions.

---

## March 25–26 | Discovery Days
**Format:** IN-PERSON (mandatory)
**Location:** Mesa Computing Module (MCM), Room 1010
**Time:** 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM (both nights)

The heart of the competition. Startup founders present at booths. VC teams rotate through, evaluating each startup. Both tracks must attend both nights.

**Dress code:** Business casual — this is a professional event.

**Founders:** Prepare to present 5–7 minute pitches + Q&A, repeatedly.

**VCs:** Bring notepads, business cards, and your sharpest questions. You're making real investment decisions.

---

## March 30 | Investment Day
**Format:** IN-PERSON
**Location:** Johnson Student Center (JSC)
**Time:** 6:30 PM – 8:30 PM

VC teams present final investment decisions. Term sheets are submitted to chosen startups. Negotiations happen live.

---

## March 31 | Final VC Submissions Due
**Deadline:** 11:59 PM Mountain Time — VC Teams Only

Upload all post-Discovery Days documents through the submissions portal:
- Deal memos (per company invested in)
- Term sheets (per company invested in)
- Due diligence notes (per company)
- Portfolio summary (one document)

---

## April 2 | VC Partner Meeting
**Format:** TBD
**Audience:** VC teams + faculty advisors

Internal review of all VC team submissions. Final scoring and deliberation.

---

## April 3 | Awards Ceremony
**Format:** IN-PERSON
**Location:** Johnson Student Center (JSC)
**Time:** 11:30 AM – 1:00 PM

Prize winners announced. Investment totals revealed. The entire Lobo startup ecosystem celebrates together.

**Come hungry — lunch is provided.**

---

*All dates subject to minor adjustment. Follow your email for any schedule updates.*
